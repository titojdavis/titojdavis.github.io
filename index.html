Student Enrichment

I work at Discovery Charter School in Student Enrichment. That means I pull students from class to work with them individually on special projects in areas of study they are proficient in. I work with students K-6th grade.

I also love to write fiction in my free time. I primarily write Science Fiction and Fantasy. I have two completed short stories, and a completed screenplay. I try to make my stories strongly based in themes and emotions rather than plot. Lately I have been working on a sci-fi novel that I have been working on, in pieces, for the greater part of three years.

I enjoy being outdoors, playing and watching sports, and of course, a good book. I like to travel, but only if I can stay a while. I've spent two months in Portugal and a year in Israel. I appreciate art in all forms from my tattoos to the art I hang in my room. Above all I love my family and friends, without whom I am nothing.
